Do you have what it takes to be a Navitas designer? Nobody says that being a Navitas designer was easy. Use your characteristics in order to succeed. In order to succeed even more, read the below essay about the steps of being a Navitas designer..

The process

You first fill in the applying form in order to go to the performance center. If you get an application email, accept the invitation and whoever gets it are required to first go to Xi An to earn a Navitas contract and train at the performance center. The coaches would accept the invitation there in the main office.

You are now readu to train at the training ground of our industry.You must attend every single one of them; if you are not able to attend, you must have a valid reason such as an illness or a dislocated elbow. You must train there in order to learn designing objects by using different materials. Designers are expected to be prepared throughout the tryout lessons. Each designer would get feedback from their respective coaches and would earn future opportunities to be in the main design team. When they get positive feedback, they would begin their designer journey at the performance center.
