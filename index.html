What We Expect

We expect determination, perseverance, self control and loyalty. Navitas designers must exceed all those four charcateristics to each other, even though if they are here to compete.

Our Youtube Channel

Not only does the Navitas company have a Youtube Channel, but the Performance Center has one too! Subscribe to the channel if you want to see how we train our designers here. This can easily make you choose to attend or not.

Follow Us

Please follow us on Twitter and on Instagram. You can easily see who would go on and become a future Navitas designer.

Note that if you haven't got a contract to Navitas, and you are a recruit, go to the Navitas Global Headquarters in XiAn, China to sign it before you attend the training lessons we have.
